{M m} and {O o}
  • letter of latin alphabet that is between [{M m},{O o}]
    • {L l} and {N n}
      • letter of latin alphabet that is between [{L l},{N n}]
    • {N n} and {P p}
      • letter of latin alphabet that is between [{N n},{P p}]
    • {N n} and [{O o},{P p},{Q q},{R r},{S s},{T t},{U u},{V v},{W w},{X x},{Y y},{Z z}]
      • {M m} and [{N n},{O o},{P p},{Q q},{R r},{S s},{T t},{U u},{V v},{W w},{X x},{Y y},{Z z}]
    • case that is lower of {N n}
    • case that is upper of {N n}
      • N has code that is represented by sum of [4,8,16,128]
      • N and [ ,n,}]
    • {N n} has name that is represented by string that consists of [{,N, ,n,}]
