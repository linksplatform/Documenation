{N n} and {P p}
  • letter of latin alphabet that is between [{N n},{P p}]
    • {M m} and {O o}
      • letter of latin alphabet that is between [{M m},{O o}]
    • {O o} and {Q q}
      • letter of latin alphabet that is between [{O o},{Q q}]
    • {O o} and [{P p},{Q q},{R r},{S s},{T t},{U u},{V v},{W w},{X x},{Y y},{Z z}]
      • {N n} and [{O o},{P p},{Q q},{R r},{S s},{T t},{U u},{V v},{W w},{X x},{Y y},{Z z}]
    • case that is lower of {O o}
    • case that is upper of {O o}
      • O has code that is represented by sum of [one,4,8,16,128]
      • O and [ ,o,}]
    • {O o} has name that is represented by string that consists of [{,O, ,o,}]
