[{T t},{V v}]

{T t} and {V v}
  • letter of latin alphabet that is between [{T t},{V v}]
    • {S s} and {U u}
      • letter of latin alphabet that is between [{S s},{U u}]
    • {U u} and {W w}
      • letter of latin alphabet that is between [{U u},{W w}]
    • {U u} and [{V v},{W w},{X x},{Y y},{Z z}]
      • {T t} and [{U u},{V v},{W w},{X x},{Y y},{Z z}]
    • case that is lower of {U u}
      • u has code that is represented by sum of [one,8,32,64,128]
      • u and }
      • u and m
      • u and p
      • u and [p,p,e,r]
    • case that is upper of {U u}
      • U has code that is represented by sum of [one,8,32,128]
      • U and [ ,u,}]
    • {U u} has name that is represented by string that consists of [{,U, ,u,}]
