[{V v},{X x}]

{V v} and {X x}
  • letter of latin alphabet that is between [{V v},{X x}]
    • {U u} and {W w}
      • letter of latin alphabet that is between [{U u},{W w}]
    • {W w} and {Y y}
      • letter of latin alphabet that is between [{W w},{Y y}]
    • {W w} and [{X x},{Y y},{Z z}]
      • {V v} and [{W w},{X x},{Y y},{Z z}]
    • case that is lower of {W w}
      • w has code that is represented by sum of [one,4,8,32,64,128]
      • w and }
      • w and [e,e,n]
      • w and [e,r]
    • case that is upper of {W w}
      • W has code that is represented by sum of [one,4,8,32,128]
      • W and [ ,w,}]
    • {W w} has name that is represented by string that consists of [{,W, ,w,}]
